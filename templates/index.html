Welcome to LabHelper

LabHelper is an application designed to precisely analyze and measure the area of corrosion from photographs taken after climatic tests.

Our tool streamlines the process of corrosion analysis, making it faster, more accurate, and easily accessible.

Key Features

  • Accurate Analysis: Utilize advanced algorithms to determine the area of corrosion with high precision.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design, enabling users to easily upload and analyze images.
  • Secure Access: Only authorized users can access the system, ensuring data confidentiality and integrity.
